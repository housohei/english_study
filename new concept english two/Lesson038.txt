Lesson 038

except
Except a broken chair, the room has no furniture. //chair 与 furniture 性质形同,同一类事物

except for
Except for a broken chair, the room is empty. //except for 置于句首，没有所指项目类别的限制

except that
I know nothing about this career except that he is a graduate of Oxford University. //我对他的职业一无所知，除了知道他是牛津大学毕业外

apart from
1)除...之外（别无）
Apart from a few faults, he is a trustworthy teacher. //除了一些小毛病之外，他是一个好老师
2)除...之外（尚有）
Apart from the cost, the dress doesn't suit me. //除了这个价格外，衣服也不适合我

but //prep.除...之外
//与no,nobody,all,anywhere等连用
Nobody knew her name but me. //除了我没有人知道他
They were all there on time but the chairman. //除了主席之外其他人都准时到场了

but //强调未被排除的其它事务
except //强调所排除之人或物

Mediterranean adj./n.
Mediterranean climate //地中海气候

complain //v.
1)v.抱怨，投诉，发牢骚
complain to sb about sth
complain to sb at sth
complain about the terrible weather
You are always complaining about your fate! //你总是抱怨命运
What was the weather like on your holiday?
->I can't complain. //好的不能再好
/It was as good as could expect.
/It couldn't be better.

complain of ... //诉说病，痛等
complain of a toothache

2)(正式地)投诉
We complained to the police about the noise from the bar. //酒吧间的噪音太大了，我们向警察投诉了

complaint //n.
1)[u]抱怨，埋怨，不满
You have no reason for complaint. //你没有什么理由好抱怨的
2)[c]申诉，控诉
They are full of complaints about their labor conditions. //关于劳动条件他们牢骚一大堆

continually //adv.频繁地，屡次地，反复地
I am continually losing things. //我总是丢东西
He was continually late for work. //他总是迟到

continuously //连续不间断地；不间断地
They chattered continuously for an hour. //他们连续不间断地唠叨了一个小时

continue //v.
1)继续，持续 //用于不中断的继续或中断后的继续
The rain continued till late at night. //雨一直持续到了半夜
The discussion continued after a break. //休息过后讨论继续

continue doing/continue to do
How can you continue to work with so much nose? //有这么大噪音你怎么能继续工作呢
/How can you continue working with so much nose?

2)继续存在或不断发生
continue + adj.
The weather continued cold. //天气依然很冷
to be continued //未完待续

continual //adj.
1)一再重复
continual complaints //频频的抱怨
continual invitations //频频的邀请
2)连续的
continual rain //连续不停的雨

continuous //时间上或空间上的连续
a continuous line of cars //络绎不绝的汽车
continuous rain //连绵不断的雨

区别
continual //指中间有间断但持续很久，好像没有停止的意味
You need continual practice.
continuous //指中间没有间断，但持续一段时间后可能会停下来
The plane is capable of two days continuous flight. //这架飞机能持续飞行两天

bitter //adj.
1)苦味的
a bitter taste //哭的味道


taste: sour, sweet, bitter, hot, salty //酸甜苦辣咸

2)痛苦的，令人悲伤的
a bitter life
a bitter experience
bitter tears
She has a bitter memory. //她有段痛苦的回忆
3)严寒的
A bitter winter.

bitterly //adv.苦苦地，惨痛地，严寒地
He was bitterly disappointed. //他极度地失望
be bitterly cold //刺骨寒冷
bitterness n.
Who goes through the happiness and bitterness with me? //谁和我同甘共苦

sunshine n.

3)快活，开朗
Her presence always gives sunshine to all of us. //他的出现总是让我们开心
a ray of sunshine //一线光明希望
