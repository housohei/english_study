/** 一般现在时与现在进行时 **/
1.表示客观事实，普遍真理用一般现在时
Two and four makes six.
Light travels faster than sound.
Water boils at 100 °C.
The moon moves round the earth.
2.表现在的特征或状态，用一般现在时
She is always ready to help others.
It all depends.
He loves sports.
3.表示经常性或习惯性的动作，用一般现在时
We always care for each other and help each other.
They cycle to work every day.
They often get up at 7 o'clock.
4.表示状态和感觉的动词通常用一般现在时较少用于进行时态
be            love             like
hate          want             hope
need          prefer           wish
know          understand       remember
believe       recognize        appear
appreciate    feel             forget
hear          look like        notice
resemble      see              think

I hear that you like classical music.
I remember Tom very well. Do you know him?
I feel a sharp pain in my chest.
You see what I mean?
5.表示状态和感觉的动词通常用一般现在时较少用于进行时态,但如果词义发生转变，能表示一个动作正在进行时可用于进行时态
Do you see anyone over there?
Are you seeing someone off? //你要送人吗?
I hear someone singing.
They're hearing an English talk. //他们正在听一个英语演讲。
What do you think of it?
What are you thinking about?
He looks well today.
He is looking after his little sister.
