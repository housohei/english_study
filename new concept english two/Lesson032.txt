/** 形容词副词的同级比较 **/
//表示 "和...一样地"
as + adj./adv. + as  //其中adj.或adv.必须用原形
//表示 "不如，不及"
not as/so + adj./adv. + as  //其中adj.或adv.必须用原形

He is not as energetic as a young man. //他不如年轻人那样有活力
Let's walk. It's just as quick as taking the bus. //我们走路吧，和乘公共汽车是一样的快
He is not as busy as before.
We'll give you as much help as we can.
Please send us the technical data as soon as possible.
He could speak English as fluently as an Englishman.
I can't speak so/as fast as you can.
I didn't do so/as well as I should.

//as...as; so...as 结构前可以用just, almost, nearly, half 等词表示程度
She hasn't been quite so unlucky as she pretends. //她并不像她所装的那样倒霉
He doesn't dress half so strangely as Tom. //他的衣着的奇异远不及汤姆

=People are less honest than they once were.

a/an 与 one 的异同
1)相同：二者都有一个的意思，有时可互换，尤其表示数量，长度，重量时
a foot = one foot
a pound = one pound
a hundred = one hundred

2)不同： a/an 表示类别， 数词one 强调数目
Can a girl do this? //这是女孩干的活吗？ --Maybe a boy can do it.
--Maybe a boy can do it.

Can one girl do this? // Maybe more girls can do it.
--Maybe more girls can do this.

3)表示数目对比的场合，用one, 不用a/an
I have one girlfriend, but you have two.
Tom has two brothers but only one sister.

4)某些习语中，a/an 与one 含义不同
at a time //每次
at one time //一度

more than a year //一年多 --如一年零一个月
more than one year //不止一年 --如三年
