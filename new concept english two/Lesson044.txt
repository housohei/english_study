/** 动名词 **/ //--20课
1.做主语
Eating is always a pleasure.

2.做介词宾语
I am very keen on cycling. //--be keen 喜欢
He sat there without saying anything.
I am looking forward to seeing him tomorrow. //--look forward to 期待
I am used to getting up late. //--be used to 习惯于

3.做动词宾语
They began running.
=They began to run.
They continued running.
=They continued to run.
//总结：有些动词后面加to do或doing含义没有什么区别 --begin / start / continued
I love sitting in the garden when it's fine.
I like sitting in the garden when it's fine.
I hate disturbing people when they're busy.
I hate to disturb you, but can I come in for a moment, please?
//总结:在有些动词后面，如like, love, hate等，后面加to do 表示目前一次性的喜好，加doing做宾语表长期性的喜好

//动名词有被动含义
My shirt is torn. It needs mending.
= It needs to be mended.
Those windows are dirty. They want washing.
=They want to be washed.
It's a difficult problem.It needs thinking about.
