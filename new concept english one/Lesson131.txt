What's worrying you? //什么事使你烦恼

worriless //无忧无虑
worrying //使人烦恼

spend...on sth //花钱（时间）在某方面
She spends a lot of money on clothes. //她把大量的钱花在买衣服上
spend ...in doing sth // 花多少时间作某事
We'll have to spend 200 dollars （in）getting our car repaired. //我们的花200美元把我们的车修好
spend + n.(时光，假日) + 地点状语
I spend my childhood in a small town. //我在一个小城镇里度过了我的童年
The story may or may not be true. //那故事也许是真的也许是假的

make sure //弄肯定，设法做到
Please make sure that the house is properly locked. //请确信房子已经锁上
takes sb. some time to do sth.
It takes a long time to travel by sea.

might 是 may 的过去式
