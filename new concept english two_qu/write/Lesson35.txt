Row used to drive a taxi, a short while ago, he became a bus driver and he has not regretted it.
Because he is finding his new work is far more exciting.
When he was driving along Ford street recently, he saw two thieves rush out of a shop and run towards a waiting car.
One of them was carrying a bag full of money.
Row acted quickly and drove the bus straight at the thieves.
The one with money got such a fright that he dropped the the bag.
As the thieves was trying to get away in their car, Row drove his bus struck into the back of it.
While the battered car was moving away, Row stopped his bus and telephoned the police.
The thieves' car was badly damaged and easy to recognize.
Shortly afterwards, the police stopped the car and both men were arrested.
