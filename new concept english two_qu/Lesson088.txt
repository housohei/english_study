if 虚拟条件状语从句可省略if，但要倒装
If you had joined us, we would have had more fun.
→Had you joined us, we would have had more fun.
If there had not been a hard layer of rock beneath the soil, they would have completed the job in a few hours.
→Had there not been a hard layer of rock beneath the soil, they would have completed the job in a few hours.
